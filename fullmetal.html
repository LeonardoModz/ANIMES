Fullmetal Alchiemist : Brotherhood


     "Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood" is an anime series adapted from 
Hiromu Arakawa's manga. The story follows two brothers, Edward and
Alphonse Elric, who use alchemy in their quest to restore their bodies
after a failed attempt to bring their mother back to life. The brothers
embark on a journey to uncover the Philosopher's Stone, encountering
political intrigue, moral dilemmas, and powerful adversaries.
